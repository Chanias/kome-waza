Kome Waza

( kõ-meh wa-za )

Respect the fish! Kome Waza strives to provide you with a
dedicate balance between time-honored Japanese sushi
traditions and exiciting new Asian-inspired flavors in a
refreshing new take on the East Coast poke trend.

SIGNATURE POKE

   1  WHITE SOY AVOCADO CREAM BOWL

Seared tuna over 8-grain brown rice mixed greens, hijiki quinoa, white soy avocado
cream sauce, sesame seeds, garlic chips, kizami nori, tobiko, and salsa.

2  SILKEN SHISO TOFU BOWL

Tuna and tofu over 8-grain brown rice with mixed greens, hijiki quinoa, edamame,
silken shiso tofu sauce, avocado, and kizami nori.

3  SALTED MANGO HAMACHI BOWL

Hamchi and scallops over mixed greens with mango, hijiki quinoa, edamane, tomato,
mango anchovy sauce, sesame seeds, and radish sprouts.

4  POMEGRANATE CITRUS SPICE BOWL

Salmon over sushi rice with mixed greens, tomato, sweet onion, cucumber, edamane,
pomegranate citrus spice sauce, ogo seaweed, sesame seeds, radish sprouts, and tobiko.

5  WASABI CASHEW TAKO BOWL

Octopus, scallops, and shrimp over kale cold noodles with mixed greens, hijiki quinoa,
tomato, wasabi cashew peanut sauce, ogo seaweed, rice pop, and radish sprouts.
